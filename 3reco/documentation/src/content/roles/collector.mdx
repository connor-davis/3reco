# Collector Users

Collectors are the sellers on the 3rEco platform. They gather recyclable materials and sell them to businesses. In the app, collectors have a single focused view: their **Dashboard**, which tracks all their sales activity.

## Onboarding

Collectors complete their profile with:

- First name, last name
- ID number
- Contact details (phone, address, city, province, area code)
- Acceptance of terms

`profileComplete` is set to `true` after the form is submitted, unlocking access to the full app.

## What Collectors Can Access

Collectors see only the **Dashboard**, notifications, and their profile. They do not have access to the Marketplace, Stock, or Transactions pages.

| Page | Available to Collectors |
|---|---|
| Dashboard | ✅ |
| Market (browse / requests) | ❌ |
| Stock management | ❌ |
| Collections | ❌ |
| Transactions | ❌ |
| Notifications | ✅ |
| Profile | ✅ |

## Collector Dashboard

The collector dashboard shows revenue and sales analytics for all sales where they were the seller:

| Stat | Calculation |
|---|---|
| **Total Revenue** | Sum of `price × weight` for all sales |
| **Total Volume Sold** | Sum of `weight` for all sales (kg) |
| **Carbon Savings** | Sum of `weight × material.carbonFactor` |

A **daily sales line chart** shows volume sold per day over the last 30 days. A **latest sales table** shows the 5 most recent transactions with material, buyer, weight, price, and date.

Date range filtering is available for all stats.

## How Collector Sales Are Recorded

Collectors do not initiate trades in the UI. Their sales are recorded by businesses via a **direct purchase**: a business goes to the Transactions page and clicks **Record purchase**, selecting the collector, material, weight, and price. This creates a `c2b` transaction immediately and the collector sees it on their dashboard.

## Transaction Record

After a c2b sale is recorded:

- A `transaction` document is created with `type: "c2b"` and the collector's `_id` as `sellerId`
- A PDF invoice is generated and stored in Convex file storage
- The collector's dashboard stats update in real time

<Callout type="tip" title="How do I know when I have a sale?">
  You will receive a notification when a business records a purchase from you. Your dashboard stats and sales table update immediately — no refresh needed.
</Callout>
