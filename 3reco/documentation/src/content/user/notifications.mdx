# Notifications

3rEco sends you real-time notifications whenever something important happens. The notification types you receive depend on your account role.

## The Notification Bell

The **bell icon** in the top navigation bar shows a badge with the count of **unread** notifications. Click it to open the notification panel.

## Types of Notifications

| Type | Who receives it | When |
|---|---|---|
| **New request received** | Business (seller) | A buyer sent a request for one of your listed stock items |
| **Offer received** | Business (buyer) | A seller responded to your request with an offer |
| **Offer accepted** | Business (seller) | The buyer accepted your offer — transaction complete |
| **Offer declined** | Business (seller) | The buyer declined your offer |
| **Offer withdrawn** | Business (buyer) | The seller withdrew their offer |
| **Request rejected** | Business (buyer) | The seller declined your purchase request |
| **Request cancelled** | Business (seller) | A buyer cancelled their request to you |
| **Stock listed** | Business | Your stock item is now visible on the marketplace |
| **Stock unlisted** | Business | Your stock item has been removed from the marketplace |
| **Purchase recorded** | Collector | A business has recorded a purchase from you |

## Reading a Notification

Click any notification to:

- Mark it as **read**
- Follow the link to the relevant page (e.g. the request detail, your stock list)

Unread notifications are highlighted. Once clicked they are marked read automatically.

## Marking All as Read

Click **Mark all as read** at the top of the notification panel to clear the unread badge without opening each one individually.

## Dismissing Notifications

Click the **✕** on a notification to **dismiss** it. Dismissed notifications are permanently hidden from your panel — they do not appear again.

Click **Dismiss all** to clear the entire panel at once.

<Callout type="tip">
  Dismissing a notification does not affect the underlying request, transaction, or stock item — it only removes the notification from your feed.
</Callout>

## Real-Time Updates

Notifications arrive in real time. You do not need to refresh the page — the bell badge and notification list update automatically as events occur on the platform.
