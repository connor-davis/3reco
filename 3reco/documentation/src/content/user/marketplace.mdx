# Marketplace

The **Marketplace** is where buyers discover available stock and initiate purchase requests. Any listed stock from other users appears here.

<Callout type="note">
  The Marketplace is available to **Business**, **Staff**, and **Admin** accounts. Collectors do not have marketplace access — businesses record purchases from collectors directly via the [Transactions](/docs/user/transactions) page.
</Callout>

## Browsing Listings

Navigate to **Market** in the sidebar to see all currently listed stock. Each card shows:

- **Material name** (e.g. HDPE Plastic, Cardboard)
- **Weight available** (kg)
- **Price per kg** asked by the seller
- **Seller name**

Listings are sorted by newest first and load in pages — scroll down to load more.

## Sending a Purchase Request

When you find a listing you are interested in:

1. Click **Request** on the listing card
2. The seller receives a notification that someone is interested in their stock
3. A new request is created with status **Pending** and appears in your [Outgoing requests](/docs/user/negotiations#outgoing-requests)

<Callout type="tip" title="What happens next?">
  The seller reviews your request and either makes you a counter-offer (specifying weight and price) or rejects it. See [Negotiations](/docs/user/negotiations) for the full flow.
</Callout>

## Viewing Your Outgoing Requests

Go to **Market → Outgoing** to see all requests you have sent. Each entry shows:

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Material | The recyclable material requested |
| Seller | Who you sent the request to |
| Status | Current stage of the request |
| Offer | Weight and price offered by the seller (once they respond) |

Click any row to open the request detail page where you can accept, decline, or cancel.

## Request Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Sent to seller, waiting for them to respond |
| **Offered** | Seller has made you an offer — action required |
| **Accepted** | You accepted the offer; transaction is complete |
| **Rejected** | Seller declined your request |
| **Cancelled** | You cancelled the request |

## Cancelling a Request

You can cancel any request that is still **Pending** or in **Offered** state:

1. Open the request from **Market → Outgoing**
2. Click **Cancel request**
3. The seller is notified and the request is closed

Once a request is **Accepted** or **Rejected** it cannot be cancelled.
