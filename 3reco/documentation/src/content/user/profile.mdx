# Your Profile

The **Profile** page lets you view and update your personal and business details. Keeping your profile accurate ensures your trading partners can identify you correctly.

## Accessing Your Profile

Click your name or avatar in the navigation, then select **Profile** — or navigate directly to `/profile`.

## Editable Fields

### Personal details

| Field | Notes |
|---|---|
| **First name** | Your given name |
| **Last name** | Your family name |
| **ID number** | South African national ID number |
| **Phone** | Contact number visible to the platform |

### Address

| Field | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Street address** | Physical street address |
| **City** | City or town |
| **Area code** | Postal code |
| **Province** | One of the nine South African provinces |

### Business details *(Business accounts only)*

| Field | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Business name** | Trading name shown to other users in listings and transactions |
| **Business registration number** | CIPC registration number |

## Saving Changes

Click **Save** after editing any field. Changes take effect immediately.

<Callout type="warning" title="Email cannot be changed here">
  Your email address is tied to your authentication account. To change it, use the account settings provided by your sign-in method (e.g. Google account settings, or contact support).
</Callout>

## Account Type

Your account type (Collector, Business, Admin, or Staff) is shown on your profile but **cannot be changed by you**. If you need your account type changed, contact an administrator.

<Callout type="note">
  Changing your account type would affect what you can see and do on the platform — it is an admin action to prevent misuse.
</Callout>

## Profile Completeness

A profile is considered **complete** when all required fields are filled in and you have agreed to the Terms of Service. An incomplete profile blocks access to the marketplace, stock management, and transactions.

If you see a banner prompting you to complete your profile, fill in any missing fields and save — the banner disappears automatically once all required information is present.
