# Managing Your Stock

The **Stock** page lets you record the recyclable materials you have on hand, set your asking price, and choose whether to display each item on the public marketplace.

<Callout type="note">
  Stock management is available to **Collectors** and **Businesses**. Admins and Staff can view stock but do not list items directly.
</Callout>

## Adding a Stock Item

1. Go to **Stock** in the navigation
2. Click **Add stock**
3. Select the **material** from the dropdown (e.g. PET Plastic, Cardboard, Aluminium Cans)
4. Enter the **weight** in kilograms
5. Set your **asking price** per kilogram

<Callout type="warning" title="Minimum price">
  Your asking price must be **at or above the base price** set by the platform for that material. You will see an error if your price is too low.
</Callout>

6. Click **Save** — the stock item is created but **not yet visible** on the marketplace

## Editing a Stock Item

Click the **edit** icon (pencil) next to any stock entry to update:

- **Weight** — adjust if you have sold some stock outside the platform or received more
- **Price per kg** — revise your asking price at any time (still subject to the minimum)

Click **Save** to apply changes.

## Listing Stock on the Marketplace

Stock is hidden by default. To make it visible to buyers:

1. Find the stock item you want to list
2. Toggle the **Listed** switch to **On**

The item immediately appears in the public marketplace. You will receive a notification confirming it is live.

To remove it from the marketplace, toggle the switch back to **Off** — buyers will no longer be able to send you requests for that item.

<Callout type="tip" title="Automatic unlisting">
  If a transaction completes and your stock weight drops to zero, the item is automatically unlisted so buyers are not shown unavailable stock.
</Callout>

## Deleting a Stock Item

Click the **delete** icon (bin) next to a stock entry to remove it permanently. This does not affect any transaction history.

<Callout type="warning">
  You cannot delete a stock item that has pending or active transaction requests. Resolve or cancel those requests first.
</Callout>

## Stock Status at a Glance

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Unlisted** | Only visible to you — not on the marketplace |
| **Listed** | Visible to all buyers on the marketplace |

The weight shown always reflects your current holdings. When a transaction is completed the platform automatically deducts the traded weight.
