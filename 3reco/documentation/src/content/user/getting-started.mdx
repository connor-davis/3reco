# Getting Started

Welcome to **3rEco** — the recycling marketplace that connects collectors with businesses. This guide walks you through creating your account, completing your profile, and getting ready to use the platform.

## Create an Account

1. Open 3rEco and click **Sign in / Register**
2. Enter your email address and choose a password, or continue with Google
3. Verify your email — check your inbox for a confirmation link

<Callout type="tip" title="Already have an account?">
  Skip straight to [signing in](#sign-in) below — no need to go through setup again.
</Callout>

## Sign In

Enter your email and password on the login screen. If you forgot your password, click **Forgot password** to receive a reset link by email.

## Complete Your Profile

After your first sign-in you will be prompted to complete your profile before accessing the platform.

### Step 1 — Choose your account type

| Type | Who it is for |
|---|---|
| **Collector** | Individuals or small operations that collect and sell recyclable materials |
| **Business** | Companies that buy recyclables, process them, or resell them |

<Callout type="note">
  Admin and Staff accounts are assigned by an administrator — you cannot self-select these roles during registration.
</Callout>

### Step 2 — Personal details

Fill in:

- **First name** and **Last name**
- **ID number** (South African national ID)
- **Phone number**

### Step 3 — Address

Provide your:

- **Street address**
- **City**
- **Area code**
- **Province** (one of the nine South African provinces)

### Step 4 — Business details *(Business accounts only)*

- **Business name** — the trading name shown to other users
- **Business registration number** — your CIPC registration number

### Step 5 — Agree to terms

Read and accept the Terms of Service to activate your account.

<Callout type="warning" title="Profile must be complete">
  You will not be able to access the dashboard or any platform features until your profile is fully completed and you have accepted the terms.
</Callout>

## What Happens Next

Once your profile is complete you will land on your **Dashboard**. From there you can:

- **Collectors** → Your [Dashboard](/docs/user/dashboard) shows your sales summary — revenue, volume sold, and carbon savings. Businesses record purchases from you directly (either as a direct purchase or via the marketplace), so sales will appear here once a business transacts with you.
- **Businesses** → [Browse the marketplace](/docs/user/marketplace) or [record direct purchases](/docs/user/transactions) from collectors
- All users → Check [notifications](/docs/user/notifications) and review your [profile](/docs/user/profile) at any time
