# Understanding Account Roles

3rEco has four account types, each with a different set of capabilities. This page explains what each role can and cannot do.

## Role Overview

| Feature | Collector | Business | Staff | Admin |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Add & manage stock | ❌ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |
| List stock on marketplace | ❌ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Browse marketplace | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Send purchase requests | ❌ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Respond to requests (sell) | ❌ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Record direct purchases (c2b) | ❌ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |
| View own transactions | ✅ (dashboard only) | ✅ (sales + expenses) | ✅ (all) | ✅ (all) |
| Export CSV | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Dashboard stats | Personal sales | Personal | Platform-wide | Platform-wide |
| Manage users | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Manage materials | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |

---

## Collector

Collectors are individuals or small operations that gather recyclable materials and sell them to businesses.

**What collectors see on 3rEco:**

- **Dashboard only** — showing their personal sales summary (total revenue, volume sold, and carbon savings)
- Notifications and profile settings

Collectors do not have access to the Marketplace, Stock management, or the Transactions page. Their sales are recorded by businesses — either as a direct purchase (off-platform) or via a completed marketplace negotiation between businesses. Once a business records a purchase from a collector, that sale appears on the collector's dashboard.

**Collector sales are always c2b (collector → business).** Collectors do not purchase from other users.

---

## Business

Businesses buy recyclable materials — from collectors or from other businesses — to process or resell.

**What businesses do on 3rEco:**

- Browse the marketplace and send purchase requests to sellers
- Accept offers from sellers to complete trades via the marketplace
- Record walk-in or direct purchases from collectors without going through the marketplace
- List surplus stock on the marketplace for other businesses to buy (b2b trades)
- Track spend, volume, stock holdings, and carbon savings on their dashboard

---

## Staff

Staff members have a read-only and oversight role. They can see platform-wide activity but cannot initiate trades.

**What staff can do:**

- View the admin dashboard with platform-wide stats
- View all transactions across the platform
- Export transaction data

Staff cannot manage users, add materials, or conduct any trades.

---

## Admin

Admins have full platform access including user and content management.

**What admins can do — in addition to staff capabilities:**

- **Manage users** — view all registered users, change any user's account type, or remove an account
- **Manage materials** — create, edit, and delete material types (e.g. add a new recyclable category with its base price and carbon factor)
- View platform-wide stats and all transactions

<Callout type="warning" title="Admin privileges">
  Admins cannot change their own account type or delete their own account — these safeguards prevent accidental lock-out.
</Callout>

---

## How Role Assignment Works

- **Collector** and **Business** are self-selected during profile setup
- **Staff** and **Admin** are assigned by an existing Admin via the **Admin → Users** page
- Once assigned, your role is shown on your profile but only an Admin can change it

If you believe your account has the wrong role, contact your platform administrator.
