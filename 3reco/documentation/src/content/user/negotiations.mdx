# Negotiations

Every purchase on 3reco goes through a negotiation flow. This page explains each stage from the buyer's initial request through to a completed transaction — and what each party can do at every step.

## How It Works

```
Buyer sends request  →  Seller reviews  →  Seller makes offer
       ↓                                         ↓
  [Pending]                            [Offered — buyer reviews]
                                            ↓              ↓
                                      [Accepted]      [Declined → back to Pending]
                                          ↓
                                  Transaction created
                                  Invoice generated
```

Both parties can also exit at any stage (cancel / reject / withdraw).

---

## If You Are the Buyer

### Viewing incoming offers (Outgoing requests)

Go to **Market → Outgoing** to see all requests you have sent and their current status.

### When a seller makes you an offer

You will receive a **notification** and the request status changes to **Offered**. Open the request to see:

- The **weight** (kg) the seller is willing to trade
- The **price per kg** they are asking

You have three options:

| Action | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Accept offer** | Completes the transaction immediately. Stock is transferred, an invoice is generated, and the request closes as Accepted. |
| **Decline offer** | Sends the request back to Pending. The seller can make a revised offer or reject the request entirely. |
| **Cancel request** | Closes the request entirely. The seller is notified. |

### After accepting

- The traded weight is deducted from the seller's stock and added to your stock
- A PDF invoice is generated automatically and available on the [Transactions](/docs/user/transactions) page
- If the seller's remaining stock reaches zero, their listing is automatically removed from the marketplace

---

## If You Are the Seller

### Viewing incoming requests (Incoming requests)

Go to **Market → Incoming** to see all requests buyers have sent you.

### Responding to a request

Open a **Pending** request. You have two options:

**Make an offer:**

1. Click **Make offer**
2. Enter the **weight** (kg) you can supply — must not exceed your current stock
3. Enter your **price per kg** — must be at or above the material's base price
4. Click **Submit offer**

The buyer is notified immediately and can accept or decline.

**Reject the request:**

Click **Reject** to decline without making an offer. The buyer is notified that their request was not accepted.

### Managing an active offer

Once you have made an offer (status = **Offered**), you can **Withdraw offer** to pull it back. The request returns to **Pending** and the buyer is notified — you can then submit a different offer or wait.

### After the buyer accepts

The transaction completes automatically:

- Your stock is reduced by the agreed weight (unlisted if it hits zero)
- The buyer's stock is increased
- An invoice is generated for both parties

---

## Using the Chat

Every request has a **message thread** where buyer and seller can communicate freely before and after an offer is made. Use it to:

- Clarify material quality or condition
- Discuss logistics and collection arrangements
- Ask questions before committing to an offer

Messages update in real time — you do not need to refresh.

<Callout type="tip" title="Stay in touch">
  Even after accepting, the chat history is preserved so you and your trading partner can refer back to any agreements made during negotiation.
</Callout>

## Status Reference

| Status | Who can act | Available actions |
|---|---|---|
| Pending | Seller | Make offer, Reject |
| Pending | Buyer | Cancel |
| Offered | Buyer | Accept, Decline, Cancel |
| Offered | Seller | Withdraw offer, Reject |
| Accepted | — | Read-only |
| Rejected | — | Read-only |
| Cancelled | — | Read-only |
